# tabak++ Premium Vitality Tracker

**tabak++** is a high-performance, mindfulness-driven tracking application designed for individuals seeking to optimize their habits and monitor their biological recovery. Built with a focus on fluidity, design consistency, and deep personal insight, it provides a premium experience for habit management.

## 🚀 Core Features

### 1. Advanced Dashboard (Tracker)
*   **Dynamic Counter Cards**: Modern interfaces for different archetypes (Cigarettes, Joints, or Simple Counters) with physics-based animations.
*   **Vitality Progress**: A real-time header showing daily consumption vs. your personal limits with an animated "burn" effect.
*   **Quick Interaction**: Physics-based tap feedback (`bounceClick`) and long-press gestures for instant tracker editing.
*   **Custom Ordering**: Complete control over tracker arrangement on your main screen.

### 2. Biological Recovery Hub (Health)
*   **Real-Time Healing**: Watch your body repair itself in real-time. Milestones range from immediate heart rate normalization (20 mins) to long-term stroke risk reduction (5 years).
*   **Dynamic Vitality Bars**: Shimmering, glowing progress indicators that symbolize active physiological repair.
*   **Nutritional Support**: Integrated guidance for each recovery phase, providing specific advice on what to eat to support your body's healing process.
*   **Scientific Logic**: Transparent calculation markers explaining the data behind your recovery percentages.

### 3. Analytics & Trends (Vault)
*   **Trend Analysis**: High-fidelity line charts with Bezier curves showing usage patterns over 7 days or 5 weeks.
*   **Usage Heatmap**: A 24-hour visual breakdown identifying your peak activity times and potential "high-risk" hours.
*   **Performance Metrics**: Track your Streak (consistency), Economic Impact (spent/saved), and Vitality Cost (estimated health impact).
*   **Expandable History**: A organized, searchable vault of all past daily logs.

### 4. Specialized Modes & Privacy
*   **Night Owl Mode**: Manually control when your "tracking day" resets. Perfect for non-traditional schedules where the session continues past midnight.
*   **Identity Alias**: Personalize your profile with an alias and a custom profile picture.
*   **Data Sovereignty**: Full support for cloud synchronization (Firebase) with a "Proceed Offline" option for guest users.

## 💎 Premium Design Language

*   **Obsidian Theme**: A deep, high-contrast dark mode designed for visual comfort and elegance.
*   **Accent Spectrum**: Fully customizable UI colors (Lime, Emerald, Rose, Orange, Violet, Pink).
*   **90Hz+ Optimization**: Every animation and scroll is tuned for high-refresh-rate devices like the Google Pixel.
*   **Tactile Physics**: Custom spring-based interactions provide physical "weight" and feedback to every touch.

## 🛠 Technical Stack

*   **UI Engine**: Jetpack Compose (Modern Declarative UI)
*   **Architecture**: MVVM with Clean Architecture principles
*   **Local Database**: Room (SQLite) with multi-version migration safety
*   **Cloud Backend**: Firebase Firestore & Firebase Auth
*   **Image Loading**: Coil (Asynchronous Image Processing)
*   **Dependency Injection**: Hilt (Dagger)
*   **Persistence**: Jetpack DataStore (Preferences)
